Special Interview

Q. How do you usually spend your time?
It is my daily routine to relax and take it easy at the Progate office in Shibuya, Japan. It is quite comfortable there. We are free to play music as loudly as we please. But I feel most satisfied when thinking of ways to promote Progate in the lounge area.
Q. Do you really know how to program? What is your preferred programming language?
Well, I have completed every Progate lesson. And, I am considering becoming a professional software engineer, so I have purchased a Wankobook Pro.
Ruby is my favorite language. It is my desire to create web apps using Rails.
Q. What kind of food do you like? Dog food, I presume?
I am very fond of ramen. Fortunately, there are many wonderful ramen shops near the office.
Dog food does not suit me. Freshly cooked food is many times more delicious.
Q. Last question, could you tell us about what's next for you?
I will continue to promote Progate. Also, working on the LINE stamps. My allowance is comes from the LINE stamp sales. So, if everyone bought them, it would make me very happy.
